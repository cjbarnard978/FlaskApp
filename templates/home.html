Mission, Myth, and Monasticism: Religious Archetypes and Cultural Transmission between Gaelic Ireland and Scotland, 1200-1500

Welcome to the visualization page for "Mission, Myth, and Monasticism: Religious Archetypes and Cultural Transmission between Gaelic Ireland and Scotland, 1200-1500." This project uses Digital Humanities techniques to examine the spatiality of cultural exchange between Scotland and Gaelic Ireland, comparing early medieval conversion efforts with monastic correspondence in the 13th century until the Reformation.
